Virtual Reality Game

Step into a mystical world with magical elements and interactive gameplay designed using Unity. Solve puzzles, explore magical artifacts, and bake a legendary cookie in this enchanting VR escape room adventure.

The Enchanted Forest Magician’s Retreat

  • Software: Unity
  • Language: C#
Inspiration

This VR escape room draws inspiration from Studio Ghibli films like Kiki’s Delivery Service and Spirited Away, and the magical intrigue of Harry Potter's Diagon Alley.

Overview

Players become a magician living in a cozy forest retreat, blending magic and baking. Discover enchanting ingredients, spellbinding recipes, and magical artifacts in a rustic and mystical setting.

Objective

Explore the retreat, solve puzzles, and use magical ingredients to bake a legendary cookie, unlocking secrets to escape the retreat.

Aesthetic

The retreat combines rustic warmth with mystical allure, featuring hand-crafted details, warm colors, and a storybook charm.
